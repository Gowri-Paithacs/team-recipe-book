# Upma
## Ingredients
- Suji
- water
- onion
- green chillies
- Mustard Seeds
- Curry Leaves
## Steps
1. First Dry roast the suji and keep it aside
2. Add a Teaspoon oil And add the chopped onion , green Chillies , Curry Leaves
3. After that add boil and salt and give it a boil
4. Let it cook and thicken then ready to serve.
